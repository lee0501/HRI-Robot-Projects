About Us

Fusing digital precision with physical expression to create systems that connect emotionally.

We are Lee Yun-Ping, Chang Ching-Min, and Wang Yu-Tung, students in the Master's Program of Digital Content and Technologies at National Chengchi University(NCCU).

Driven by curiosity and a passion for innovation, we explore the intersection of design, technology, and human experience. Through research, storytelling, and interactive design, we aim to create digital experiences that are both meaningful and impactful.
