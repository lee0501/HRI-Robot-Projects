Project 02 —
Cinema Chatbot

POPI

An AI-powered cinema chatbot designed to act as a warm, movie-loving companion rather than a rigid customer service agent.

Overview

POPI is an interactive cinema service chatbot designed for VIESHOW Cinemas. By combining a character persona with conversational assistance, POPI delivers an engaging customer support experience. Through real-time cinema information and friendly guidance, the chatbot helps users seamlessly navigate their entire moviegoing journey — from discovering showtimes to exploring in-theater amenities.

Background

Moviegoers frequently seek immediate information regarding showtimes, screening formats, ticketing policies, and theater services. However, traditional cinema websites often require users to navigate through multiple dense pages to find simple answers. Using VIESHOW Cinemas as our project context, we explored how a conversational interface could streamline information access, while seeking ways to combine functionality with emotional design to deliver a better user experience.

Concept

A Friendly Movie Companion

The chatbot character is inspired by the most iconic symbol of the moviegoing experience — popcorn. By transforming this familiar cinema symbol into a friendly mascot, POPI establishes an immediate connection with users.

To reinforce its identity, POPI was developed with a clear character persona:

  • Role: Movie Sprite
  • Personality: Professional, Enthusiastic, and Friendly
  • Mission: To guide and support users throughout their moviegoing journey

Four Core Service Categories

🕒

Check screening schedules by date and location, filtering options based on user preferences.

🎟️

🍿

Explore popcorn flavors, combo meals, beverages, and learn about available cinema facilities.

Architecture

System Design & Flow


To achieve an intelligent and responsive experience, POPI was developed using OpenAI’s My GPTs framework, leveraging its configuration capabilities to transform a general language model into a specialized cinema assistant.

Instead of relying on static, predefined answers, the chatbot's architecture utilizes custom GPT Actions to connect directly with VIESHOW’s official database via API integration. This setup enables POPI to fetch real-time, dynamic information—including fluid showtimes, live seat availability, and active promotions—ensuring that every conversational interaction is both contextually relevant and highly precise.

Iterations

Encountered Problems & Iterative Design

Problem 01

Constraining LLM Versatility for Service Focus

Due to LLM’s vast general knowledge, they naturally tend to answer any query. Early testing revealed that without precise prompts, this versatility allowed the chatbot to address out-of-scope questions, drifting away from VIESHOW’s core service.

To resolve this, we implemented strict behavioral guardrails within the system instructions, confining POPI’s scope to cinema-related topics through polite, on-brand redirections.

Problem 02

Balancing Constraints with Persona Alignment

As system prompts became more complex to enforce boundaries, POPI began losing its personality, resulting in robotic responses that compromised the UX.

We resolved this by restructuring the prompt hierarchy, separating behavioral constraints from persona guidelines to ensure POPI remains secure while maintaining its enthusiastic, friendly tone.

Refection

Developing POPI highlighted the delicate balance between systemic accuracy and character personality. Initially, we prioritized reliability through strict conversational rules, which minimized errors but resulted in rigid, robotic interactions.

This challenge led to a pivotal realization: a chatbot is a living service touchpoint, not just an information retrieval tool. Users do not simply seek answers; building a connection between the user and the brand is equally essential.

Through this project, we learned that exceptional conversational UX requires looking beyond pure system logic to design for human emotion, and seamlessly weaving service into a narrative that feels natural, engaging, and fundamentally trustworthy.
